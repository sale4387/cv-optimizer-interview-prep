emerchantpay is a leading global payment service provider and acquirer for online, mobile, in-store and over the phone payments. Our global payments solution is available through a simple integration, offering a diverse range of features, including global acquiring, global and local payment methods, advanced fraud management and performance optimisation. We empower businesses to design seamless and engaging payment experiences for their consumers.


To support our continued growth in the Netherlands, we are looking for an experienced Business Development Manager to join our Amsterdam team.

The ideal candidate combines strong payments expertise with commercial acumen and understands how to build trusted, long-term relationships with merchants, platforms, and technology partners.


You will own the full sales cycle from market analysis and prospecting through to negotiation, implementation, and successful go-live.

Develop and execute a targeted business development strategy for the Dutch and European market
Identify, qualify, and close new enterprise opportunities across digital commerce sectors

Develop ROI-driven proposals and commercial business cases
Manage pipeline forecasting and opportunity tracking in Salesforce
Identify opportunities to expand existing merchant relationships (cross-sell / upsell)
Collaborate closely with Product, Risk, Compliance, Operations, and Implementation teams
Represent emerchantpay at industry events and within relevant partner networks

3+ years of experience in payments, acquiring, PSP, fintech, or related financial services
Strong understanding of card acquiring, online payment processing, and payment ecosystems
Experience selling into digital commerce, subscription, gaming, crypto, FX, or similar online industries is highly preferred
Proven track record in managing complex B2B sales cycles and closing enterprise deals
Strong consultative selling approach with experience engaging senior decision makers
Commercially driven with excellent negotiation and communication skills
Ability to work independently while collaborating effectively across international teams
High level of integrity, professionalism, and strong reputation in the payments industry
